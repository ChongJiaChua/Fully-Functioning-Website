Our SSLC build the bridges which connect students and staff, facilitate feedbacks from both students and staff and try our best to resolve any problems faced.
We ensure your voice is heard which improving the educational experience for all.

WHAT WE DO

Feedbacks

We provide a platform for students to share their feedback on courses and teaching methods which ensures that their concerns are being addressed.

Wellbeing

We advocate for student well-being while addressing matters like mental health support and student services.

Representative Voice

Our committee comprises dedicated student and staff representatives which ensures a range of perspectives and inclusive decision-making.
