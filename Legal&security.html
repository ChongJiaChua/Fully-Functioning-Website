Legal & Security

Legal Issues

All the pages in my website includes my name, the year and Copyright. The images used in all my webpages are copyright free and licensed. The data collected in my feedback forms meets all the specification of the GDPR. The feedback form makes clear who and why we are collecting the data as well as how their data will be handled. Users are also informed and asked consent from about the use of any cookies or tracking tools before actually collecting their data.

Security

The data collected is encrypted which prevents hackers from stealing any of the information. Every part of the user data is interpreted as a string primitive and not a control character. Once the data is collected, it is stored in the cloud instead of a local server as it is much safer and immediately backed up. Only authorised members of the SSLC will be able to access the data in order to keep the information confidential. This maintains the integrity of the data as only authorised people is able to access or change it. Only necessary data that is required will be asked from the user the minimize the amount of personal data collected. Users hold full acccess over the data they provided and would be able to change or delete at any point in time.
